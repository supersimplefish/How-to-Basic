How to dribble

My simple recommendations are to practice everything with both feet. Sometimes even doing things with your weak foot twice as much is a good idea. Really focus on learning how to change pace whenever you hit a move. This is what separates the best from the average. You'll need to practice acceleration with and without the ball to master it.
